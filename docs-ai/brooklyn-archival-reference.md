## FAQ Highlights

**Matte vs Luster:** Matte feels like an "art print" (variety of textures). Luster has shine, deeper contrast, looks more photographic.

**Durability:** Giclée prints are less physically durable than darkroom prints. Best handled minimally, framed for longevity.

**Returns:** "We always guarantee our workmanship. If we make an error of any kind in the printing, we will immediately reprint free of charge."

**File enlargement:** Can generally double the size with no big quality loss at 200dpi minimum.
